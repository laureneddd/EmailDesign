Greetings Villagers,

Building Village has been a practice for many of us - a practice in showing up regularly, in taking roles, in connecting one-to-one with others, in helping others, in learning into causes. We call it a practice because we're certainly not experts, and this work has required us to get a little uncomfortable, pushing ourselves outside our comfort zone, all for good reasons.

'Food, in the end, in our own tradition, is something holy. It's not about nutrients and calories. It's about sharing. It's about honesty. It's about identity.
- Louise Fresco
