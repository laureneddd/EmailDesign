What is Village?

Village is a community of people from all walks of life that come together regularly to build strong connection through intentional conversations.
Become part of a supportive community
Become friends with new and diverse people
Have a safe space to discuss ideas and issues that are important to you
Participate in events that bring you closer to your community

What happens in a gathering?

You'll connect with your community and make new friends by having intentional and fun conversations. Sounds pretty great, right?

We ground

We begin with a brief grounding exercise led by a volunteer. It helps attendees find more focus, peace, and a strong sense of connection with themselves - and each other.

We acknowledge

It's clear that people are struggling. Village gatherings take a moment to discuss a particular struggle or injustice facing members of our community.

We connect

In the modern world, connecting with other human beings is hard. Village gatherings help facilitate those one-on-one connections by randomly pairing attendees to discuss a prompt.

We hold open space

We hold space for small group conversations and topics chosen by attendees. These can range from serious challenges to gardening practices - in a co-created space, members get to decide!
